avsar


a platform to find personalised opportunities for high school students.


we realized during our junior year of high school that we had missed out on many opportunities. to prevent others from having the same experience, we want to ensure they know what opportunities are out there.

to solve this problem, we started "avsar". avsar is a platform that helps students find opportunities relevant to them and make the most of their high school years. by helping students discover competitions, fellowships, internships, scholarships and more.

it gives personalised recommendations based on their interests, making it easy to find the right opportunities. with a simple design, exploring new options feels natural!
